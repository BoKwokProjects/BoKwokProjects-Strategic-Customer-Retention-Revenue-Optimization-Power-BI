# 📊 Strategic Customer Retention & Revenue Optimization (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project delivers an end-to-end business intelligence solution analysing sales performance, customer behaviour, and profitability using Power BI.

The dashboard focuses on identifying key drivers of revenue, margin pressure, and customer retention risk, providing actionable insights for business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- 📈 Sales & Profitability Analysis
- 👥 Customer Lifetime & Retention Analysis
- 💰 Discount Impact on Profit Margin
- 🔝 Dynamic Top-N Customer Analysis
- 🧠 Executive Summary for decision-making
- 🏗️ Star Schema Data Model (Fact & Dimension tables)

## 🧠 Business Insights

1. Furniture generated $742K revenue but only $18.5K profit, reflecting weak profitability compared to other categories and inefficient cost–price structures.

2. The West region leads with $725K sales and $108K profit, while Central records $501K sales but only $39.7K profit, indicating major efficiency gaps.

3. Out of all customers, 411 (51.8%) are inactive, while only 158 (9.9%) are active. This indicates weak retention and high customer churn.

4. Profit margin falls from 100% (no discount) to 28.1% (high discount), confirming that aggressive discounting severely erodes profitability.

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

1. Review supplier contracts, pricing, and product mix within Furniture to lift category profit above $50K annually.

2. Adopt West’s sales strategies and customer targeting in Central to increase regional profit by at least $20K–$30K per year.

3. Launch reactivation campaigns targeting inactive customers with personalised offers to increase active customers above 20% within one year.

4. Limit high-discount transactions and prioritise low-discount promotions to maintain margins above 60%.
